Research

My research lies at the intersection of computational structural biology, bioinformatics, and AI/ML. I develop multiscale models and data-driven methods to understand how proteins and peptides interact with membranes, solvents, and ligands, and to design new bioactive peptides, biologics, and food proteins with improved functionality.

I work closely with experimental collaborators in food and grain science to connect atomistic simulations, quantum chemistry, and protein language models to measurable properties such as gluten viscoelasticity, allergenicity, and antimicrobial activity.

Core research areas

AI/ML for peptide and protein function Multiscale molecular dynamics of proteins & membranes Membrane-active & antimicrobial peptides Food protein structure, processing & allergenicity Non-covalent interactions & quantum chemistry Computational drug discovery & bioinformatics platforms

Research themes

AI-enhanced peptide prediction & design

Protein language models, GNNs, deep learning.

  • Developed pLM4CPPs, a pLM-based CNN model for CPPs.
  • Building pLM4G-CPPs, a graph-learning framework.
  • Extending UniDL4BioPep for other bioactive peptides.
Key paper
pLM4CPPs, JCIM 2025.

Membrane-active peptides & biophysics

Selectivity, pore formation, lipid heterogeneity.

  • Mechanisms of Snakin-Z selectivity across membranes.
  • Melittin pore formation in different lipid environments.
  • Modeling heterogeneous bilayers and nanodomains.
Key paper
Snakin-Z, JPCB 2025.

Food protein structure & allergenicity

MD + experiments for food systems.

  • Microsecond MD of α-gliadin in ethanol–water mixtures.
  • Ovalbumin under pressure & ultrasound.
  • Frozen dough, plant proteins, rheology integration.
Key paper
α-Gliadin, Food Chemistry 2025.

Non-covalent interactions & quantum chemistry

Cation–π, aromatic interactions, ion–water.

  • Developed theoretical descriptors for NCIs.
  • Ion–water microsolvation and competing pathways.
  • Cation–π & hydrophobic binding mechanisms.
Key papers
PCCP 2021; J. Chem. Sci. 2021.

Computational drug discovery & software

Web toolkits, cheminformatics, ML.

  • Co-developed MPDS platform.
  • Contributed to A2ID 2.0 & CAD.
  • Drug repurposing, host–guest systems, herbicides.
Key papers
A2ID 2.0 (IJBM 2023), MPDS (GigaByte 2024).

Selected ongoing projects

pLM4G-CPPs: Graph learning for CPPs

Extends pLM4CPPs by combining residue embeddings and structural graphs to improve CPP classification and motif interpretability.

Melittin pore formation

Understanding transitions from surface-bound to transmembrane states across lipid environments using AA MD and enhanced sampling.

Antifreeze proteins & ice interfaces

Exploring adsorption, ice-binding modes, and thermal hysteresis using TIP4P/ICE and CHARMM force fields.

Food protein functionality

Linking MD of gluten, ovalbumin, plant proteins with experimental rheology, RVA, and baking performance.

Collaboration

I collaborate with experimentalists, computational researchers, and data scientists on protein, peptide, food, and health applications.
