Databases & Servers

MPDS COVID-19 Web Portal

An open-source drug discovery portal integrating genes, proteins, pathways, binding sites, and AI-powered compound analysis with a database of 149M molecules and 107k fragments.

GigaByte 2024 — https://doi.org/10.1101/2023.08.29.555437

MPDS TB Web Portal

An integrated computational suite for tuberculosis research, offering cheminformatics, CADD tools, and unique MPDS IDs for all compounds.

Visit Portal

Aromatic–Aromatic Interactions Database (A2ID)

A systematic analysis of π–π motifs in proteins involving Trp, Tyr, Phe, and His, advancing understanding of aromatic stabilization in protein structures.

Int. J. Biol. Macromol. 2023 — https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ijbiomac.2023.127207

Explore Database

Cation–Aromatic Database (CAD 2.0)

A comprehensive database of 27.26M cation–aromatic motifs with geometric parameters, residue preferences, and classification of cation–π vs cation–σ interactions.

Proteins 2023 — https://doi.org/10.1002/prot.26600

Explore CAD

pLM4CPPs — AI/ML Predictor for CPPs

Deep learning architecture combining protein language models and CNNs for high-accuracy cell-penetrating peptide prediction.
